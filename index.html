Bison Management Map Using QGIS

Using QGIS, I created a map showing Montana bison management zones using data from fwp.montana.gov, which is part of the official state website. The first image shows the whole state of Montana to understand the area that is managed for bison.

    The second image is a zoomed in image of the the bison management area. The purple regions on the map illustrate the current management zones. The lime green region illustrates past management zones. The other green, smaller region illustrates bison distribution that is outside the current management zone, Indian Reservations, and National Parks. The yellow regions inside of the current management regions shows hunting areas for the 2016-2017 hunting seasons.
